Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Who we are

CourtPulse is a community-driven pickleball court status app built for players at Marine Park, Brooklyn, NY. CourtPulse is operated independently and is not affiliated with NYC Parks or any government agency.

What we collect

When you use CourtPulse, we collect the following information:

  • Display name — required to identify you in the chat and reports.
  • Phone number or email — optional, provided at signup. Used only for potential future notifications about court status or app updates.
  • Chat messages — text messages you send in park-wide or court-specific chats, stored with your display name and timestamp.
  • Court reports — status updates you submit about court conditions, stored with your display name and timestamp.
  • Moderation data — if you flag a message, we record which message was flagged and who flagged it.

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your precise GPS location.
  • We do not collect payment information.
  • We do not use cookies or tracking pixels.
  • We do not run analytics or advertising software.

How we use your information

Your information is used solely to operate CourtPulse:

  • Display names appear alongside your messages and reports so other players know who contributed.
  • Contact information (if provided) may be used to send you notifications about court status and app updates. You can opt out at any time by contacting us.
  • Moderation data is used to review and address inappropriate content.

Sharing your information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties. Your data is stored securely on Supabase (our database provider) and is only accessible to CourtPulse administrators for the purpose of operating the app.

Data retention

Chat messages and court reports are retained as long as the app is operational. Deleted messages are marked as deleted in our database and their text content is removed. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of any future notifications.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at courtpulsenyc@gmail.com.

User-generated content

CourtPulse includes chat features where users can post messages visible to other users. You are solely responsible for the content you post. CourtPulse reserves the right to remove any content that violates community guidelines, is offensive, or is otherwise inappropriate. We are not liable for content posted by other users.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including using encrypted connections (HTTPS) and secure database hosting. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

Children's privacy

CourtPulse is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of CourtPulse after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact
